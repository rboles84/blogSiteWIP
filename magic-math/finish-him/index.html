What This Does — and Doesn’t — Claim

Part of what makes this trustworthy is being clear about its edges.

What the data supports

  • Documented repeat versus non-repeat endings across all 15,107 lethal variants.
  • The cash-out conditions those lines are recorded as using.
  • Behavioral differences visible in the approved aggregates — mana, cards, and setup by finish mode.
  • Real Commander Spellbook examples behind every part of the pattern.

What it doesn't prove

  • Actual game win rates — none of this measures who wins.
  • Which combo is strongest, fastest, cheapest, most resilient, or easiest to assemble.
  • That one universal "final button" exists across every deck.
  • That the ten featured lines represent the complete population.

Uncertainty categories — Source-implicit explicit terminal and Multiple terminal paths — stay visible throughout, and the rejected action-parser reading is not used anywhere on this page.
