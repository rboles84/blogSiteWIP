Why this site exists

Most QA writing is either resume theater or abstract process. I wanted a place that's neither: a working knowledge base from someone who owns release confidence in production and still ships side projects on the weekend.

Three lanes run through everything here — professional authority (QA strategy, automation, metrics), builder proof (Vox Mana and other experiments), and a way of working — how I document, hand off, and turn messy projects into reusable systems.
