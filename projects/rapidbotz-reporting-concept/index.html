Rapidbotz QA Reporting Concept

Automated QA run summaries and readable release visibility.

QA ReportingAutomation TriageHTML Reports

Problem

Automation runs can produce lots of data without producing a clear decision for teams or leaders.

Goal

Turn run results into a readable report that explains what moved, what failed, why it matters, and what action is needed.

Stack

HTML report concept, run-result ingestion, classification rules, weekly handoff structure.

Constraints

No private employer data, no exposed system names, examples must remain generalized.

What I built

A concept structure for transforming raw automation results into a leadership-friendly QA signal.

What broke

The reporting model needs disciplined failure categories or it becomes another dashboard nobody trusts.

What I learned

A good QA report should reduce ambiguity, not just visualize more metrics.
