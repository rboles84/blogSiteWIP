Recipe Flexibility App Concept

Recipe adaptation for cookware, substitutions, time, and family constraints.

Product ThinkingFoodAI Assistance

Problem

Recipes often assume you have the exact pan, ingredient, oven behavior, time, and skill level they were written for.

Goal

Help home cooks adapt recipes safely and practically when real life does not match the recipe card.

Stack

Static concept today; future app could use structured recipe parsing, substitution rules, and user constraints.

Constraints

Must avoid unsafe cooking advice, must explain uncertainty, and must keep changes simple enough for a parent cooking on a weeknight.

What I built

A concept model for cookware swaps, time adjustments, ingredient substitutions, and family-friendly constraints.

What broke

The hard part is not generating suggestions; it is knowing when a substitution changes the actual cooking method.

What I learned

Useful AI products need constraint handling more than clever responses.
